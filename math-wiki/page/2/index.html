Indiscrete topology The coarsest possible topology on a set $X$. Definition Let $X$ be a set. The indiscrete topology is $\mathcal{T} = {\emptyset, X}$. The topological space $(X, {\emptyset, X})$ is called an indiscrete space. Properties By definition, the only sets in the indiscrete topology that are open are $X$ and $\emptyset$, and these are also the only closed sets. #topology...
