Similarly, we say they are right-equivalent, written \(a \sim_R b\), if and only if \(ab\inv \in H\). The Dihedral Group

Symmetries of a polygon. Definition The dihedral group \(D_n\) is the group of symmetries of a regular \(n\)-gon.
